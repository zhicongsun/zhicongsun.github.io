Biography

  • Zhicong received his M.Eng. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) in 2023, under the guidance of Prof. Shuang-Hua Yang and Prof. Yulong Ding. Prior to that, he earned his B.Eng. degree from the Department of Communication Engineering at the Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT) in 2020. During this time, he was honored as one of the Top 10 Influential Graduates and was advised by Prof. Changjun Yu.

Research Interests

Cyber-physical systems (CPSs) and their Safety & Security Analysis.
