Research Interests

  • In the past two years, my research interests were Cyber-physical systems (CPSs), the Internet of things (IoT), and Safety & security risk analysis.

  • Now, I am looking forward to adopting methods based on Machine Learning/Data Analysis/Human-computer Interaction/Visualisation to overcome limitations in some fields of IoT. Especially in the security and safety analysis of IoT, I've clear ideas about what limitations such methods can be used to overcome, and I shared these ideas in a 24-page 2-column review article that is under review by IEEE IoT-J.

  • Additionally, any interesting topic in the field of IoT will attract me. I believe that I can overcome any challenges with my self-motivation and down-to-earth spirit.
